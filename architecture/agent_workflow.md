# AlphaScope Agent Workflow

## Step 1

User requests company analysis.

Example:

Analyze NVIDIA

---

## Step 2

Agent Orchestrator creates research session.

---

## Step 3

News Agent collects:

* News
* Press Releases
* Events

---

## Step 4

Financial Filing Agent analyzes:

* Annual Reports
* Quarterly Reports
* Investor Presentations

---

## Step 5

Sentiment Agent calculates:

* Positive
* Neutral
* Negative

scores.

---

## Step 6

Risk Agent identifies:

* Financial Risks
* Competitive Risks
* Regulatory Risks

---

## Step 7

Topic Discovery Agent identifies:

* Emerging Themes
* Market Trends

---

## Step 8

Similarity Agent identifies:

* Similar Companies
* Competitor Candidates

---

## Step 9

Report Agent generates:

* Executive Summary
* Timeline
* Risk Analysis
* Future Catalysts
* Scenario Analysis

---

## Step 10

Results stored in PostgreSQL and ChromaDB.

---

## Step 11

RAG Chat uses stored knowledge to answer user questions.
